Privacy Policy for Shorcuit

Shorcuit uses Firebase and Google Sign-In to provide authentication and cloud synchronization of workout sessions.

Information collected

  • Google account information used for sign-in
  • Workout and training session data created by users
  • Shared session invite information

How information is used

  • To provide app functionality
  • To synchronize data between devices
  • To enable session sharing features

Shorcuit does not sell personal data.
